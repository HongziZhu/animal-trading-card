Orca

Orcas have culture since their hunting techniques and vocalizations are passed down generations.

  • Scientific Name: Orcinus orca
  • Weight: 6 tons
  • Length: 23 to 32 feet
  • Habitat: All oceans and most seas

The killer whale or orca is a toothed whale belonging to the oceanic dolphin family, of which it is the largest member. Orcas have a diverse diet. They are apex predators, as there is no animal which preys on them.
